about us

INTERACTIVE together is a custom curated interactive online content used by businesses and member based organizations for children (2-18) of those employees or members' families.


We offer small class setups with world renowned educators and influencers who produce the highest quality content. Interactive content ranges from puppet shows, arts and crafts for our little ones to working with gaming influencers, yoga and learning how to build businesses for the older ones.


Companies and organizations that offer INTERACTIVE together content for their employees or members' children as a benefit have reported higher engagement rates and overall stronger company culture that puts the employee first.
